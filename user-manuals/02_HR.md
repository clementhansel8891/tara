## 1. Pulse Command — PulseDesk (`/core/hr/dashboard`)

**Purpose:** The HR operational inbox. Shows critical workforce alerts, pending actions, and team performance signals that require immediate attention.

### Reading the Pulse Dashboard
1. Navigate to **H.R. Command → Pulse Command**.
2. The dashboard displays:
   - **Active Alerts** — overdue appraisals, expiring contracts, leave backlogs
   - **Pending Approvals** — FlowGate requests assigned to your HR queue
   - **Headcount Summary** — total staff, active/inactive breakdown
   - **Attendance Snapshot** — today's attendance vs. expected headcount
3. Click any alert card to navigate directly to the relevant tool.
4. Click **Create Request** to initiate a new FlowGate approval route.

### Using the Search Bar
1. Type a name, employee ID, or action keyword in the **Search** bar at the top.
2. Matching records appear instantly.
3. Click a result to open the relevant People Core record.

## 2. Growth Matrix (`/core/hr/growth`)

**Purpose:** Workforce analytics — performance distribution, training progress, and retention risk forecasting.

### Key Panels
- **Performance Bell Curve** — distribution of performance scores across all staff
- **Training Completion Rate** — % of assigned training completed company-wide
- **Attrition Risk Heatmap** — departments with high resignation risk (based on engagement data)
- **Tenure Distribution** — workforce breakdown by years of service

### Generating a Growth Report
1. Navigate to **H.R. Command → Growth Matrix**.
2. Select a **time range** from the filter bar.
3. Review each analytics panel.
4. Click **Generate Report** → creates a formatted HR analytics PDF.
5. Click **Share to Exec** → routes the report to company leadership via FlowGate.
6. Click **Route to FlowGate** → escalates specific risks for executive review.

## 3. People Core — Employee 360 (`/core/hr/people/:id`)

**Purpose:** The full employee record — employment details, attendance, payroll history, contracts, performance reviews, and activity stream. This is the single source of truth for each employee.

### Accessing an Employee Record
1. Navigate to **H.R. Command → People Core** (the staff directory/roster loads first).
2. Search by name, employee code, or department using the **FilterBar**.
3. Click an employee row to open their **People Core** record.

### 3.1 WorkQueue — Quick Actions
At the top of every People Core record, the **WorkQueue** strip provides:

| Button | Action |
|--------|--------|
| **New Request** | Creates a custom FlowGate workflow for this employee |
| **Escalate to Admin** | Routes this record to Administrative Oversight |
| **Route to HR** | Sends a performance/payroll/contract routing to the HR FlowGate |
| **Edit Profile** | Opens a slide-out panel to update role, department, location, and status |
| **Transfer Department** | Wizard to move the employee to a new department |
| **Update Role/Rank** | Wizard to promote or change role title and salary |
| **Suspend Record** | Places the record under temporary administrative suspension |
| **Deactivate Record** | Removes active status (soft delete) |
| **Finalize Termination** | Permanent cessation of employment (requires approval) |

### 3.2 Record Tabs

#### Identity Tab
Shows core employment data:
- Employee Code, Location, Hire Date
- Base Salary, Hourly Rate, Employment Status

#### Attendance Tab
- Full attendance history table
- Filter by date or status (Present, Absent, Leave)
- Click any row to see detailed attendance entry

#### Payroll Tab
- All payroll run periods associated with this employee
- Click a period row to see the payroll detail

#### Contracts Tab
- All active and expired contracts
- Click a contract row to see terms, dates, and status

#### Performance Tab
- All review cycles, their scores, and status
- Click a review to see the full assessment detail

#### Retail Ops Tab (Retail Staff Only)
- Active shift status at the employee's assigned store
- Historical shift count
- Shift table showing open/close times and cash positions

### 3.3 Initializing a FlowGate Workflow
1. Click **Initialize FlowGate** (top-right button on People Core).
2. A modal opens with two steps:
   - **Step 1 — Request Type:** Select from PERFORMANCE, PAYROLL, CONTRACT, or TRAINING
   - **Step 2 — Destination Department:** Select HR, Finance, Legal, Operations, or Admin
3. Enter **Notes for approvers** (e.g., "Pending contract renewal — requires sign-off").
4. Click **Send to FlowGate**.
5. A confirmation alert appears: *"Workflow request for [TYPE] initialized."*

### 3.4 Editing an Employee Profile
1. Click **Edit Profile** in the WorkQueue.
2. A slide-out panel opens on the right with fields:
   - Role Title
   - Department ID
   - Location
   - Status (Active/Inactive)
3. Update the relevant fields.
4. Click **Save Changes**.

### 3.5 Transferring a Department
1. Click **Transfer Department**.
2. A multi-step wizard opens:
   - **Step 1:** Select the **Target Department** from the dropdown (loaded from org chart).
   - **Step 2:** Select the **Target Location** (if applicable).
   - **Step 3:** Enter the **Reason for Transfer** and **Effective Date**.
3. Click **Confirm Transfer**.
4. The transfer is logged in the audit trail and the employee's department is updated immediately.

### 3.6 Promoting an Employee
1. Click **Update Role/Rank**.
2. A dialog opens:
   - Enter new **Role Title**
   - Enter new **Base Salary** (if applicable)
3. Click **Confirm Promotion**.
4. The change is logged and an audit entry is created.

### 3.7 Suspending a Record
1. Click **Suspend Record** (orange button).
2. Enter the **Reason for Suspension**.
3. Click **Confirm Suspension**.
4. The employee status changes to `SUSPENDED`. Access to modules is restricted.

### 3.8 Terminating an Employee
1. Click **Finalize Termination** (red destructive button).
2. A termination confirmation dialog opens.
3. Enter the **Termination Date**, **Reason**, and **Settlement Notes**.
4. Click **Confirm Termination**.
5. The record is finalized and routed for HR closure via FlowGate.

> **⚠ Termination is irreversible once confirmed. Always verify the employee record and obtain appropriate approvals first.**

### 3.9 Activity Stream
At the bottom of People Core, the **Activity Stream** shows:
- Comments left by HR managers
- Audit events (who did what, when)
- Mentions and notifications tied to this employee

To add a comment:
1. Click the **Comment** input at the bottom of the Activity Stream.
2. Type your note.
3. Press **Enter** or click **Post**.

## 4. Org Map (`/core/hr/org-map`)

**Purpose:** Department structure visualization and headcount intelligence.

### Reading the Org Map
1. Navigate to **H.R. Command → Org Map**.
2. The page shows all departments with:
   - **Headcount** — current staff count
   - **Open Requisitions** — unfilled positions
   - **Staffing Risk** — departments with insufficient capacity
3. Click a department card to see the full staff list for that department.

### Creating a New Department
1. Click **New Department** in the WorkQueue.
2. Enter: **Department Name**, **Department Code**, **Head of Department**.
3. Click **Save Department**.

### Escalating a Staffing Risk
1. In the department view, click **Escalate Staffing Risk**.
2. A FlowGate request is created, routing to HR for recruitment action.
3. Confirm the note and submit.

### Opening a Hiring Requisition
1. Click **Open Requisition** on any understaffed department.
2. This creates a new **Job Requisition** in Talent Flow automatically.

## 5. Talent Flow — Recruitment ATS (`/core/hr/talent-flow`)

**Purpose:** Full Applicant Tracking System (ATS) for managing job requisitions, candidates, and interview pipelines.

### 5.1 Creating a Job Requisition
1. Navigate to **H.R. Command → Talent Flow**.
2. Click **Create Requisition**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Job Title**
   - **Department**
   - **Number of Openings**
   - **Required Skills**
   - **Target Start Date**
   - **Salary Range**
4. Click **Submit Requisition** → routes to Department Head for approval via FlowGate.

### 5.2 Managing the Candidate Pipeline
Once a requisition is approved:
1. The requisition appears in the **Active Requisitions** board.
2. Click a requisition to open its **Candidate Pipeline** view.
3. Candidates move through stages:
   - **Applied → Screened → Interview → Offer → Hired / Rejected**
4. Click **Add Candidate** to manually add a candidate with their CV and contact details.

### 5.3 Scheduling an Interview
1. Open a candidate record in the pipeline.
2. Click **Schedule Interview**.
3. Select: **Interview Type** (Phone, Video, In-person), **Date & Time**, **Interviewer(s)**.
4. Click **Confirm Schedule**.
5. A notification is sent to the interviewer(s).

### 5.4 Making a Hiring Decision
1. After interviews are complete, open the candidate record.
2. Click **Make Decision**: select **Extend Offer** or **Reject**.
3. For an offer:
   - Enter **Offer Salary**, **Start Date**, **Contract Type**.
   - Click **Send to FlowGate** → routes to HR Manager for final approval.
4. Once approved, click **Confirm Hire** → creates the employee record in People Core.

## 6. Skill Track — Training & Compliance (`/core/hr/skills`)

**Purpose:** Training program management and compliance tracking across the workforce.

### 6.1 Assigning Training to an Employee
1. Navigate to **H.R. Command → Skill Track**.
2. Click **Assign Training**.
3. Select:
   - **Employee** (or use **Bulk Assign** for multiple)
   - **Training Program** from the library
   - **Due Date**
4. Click **Confirm Assignment**.
5. The employee is notified and the training appears in their record.

### 6.2 Bulk Training Assignment
1. Click **Bulk Assign** in the WorkQueue.
2. Select a **Department** or individual employees using checkboxes.
3. Select the **Training Program**.
4. Set a **Due Date**.
5. Click **Assign to All Selected**.

### 6.3 Tracking Compliance Status
- The **Compliance Dashboard** shows overall completion percentages by department.
- Red rows indicate **overdue** or **not started** training.
- Click **Export Compliance** → download a CSV of all training status for auditing.
- Click **Escalate** → sends compliance gaps to the HR Manager via FlowGate.

### 6.4 Adding a New Training Program
1. Click **New Program** in the WorkQueue.
2. Enter: **Program Name**, **Category** (Safety, Technical, Compliance, Leadership), **Duration**, **Description**.
3. Click **Save Program**.

## 7. Pay Cycle Studio (`/core/hr/payroll`)

**Purpose:** Payroll preparation, review, and approval before submission to Finance.

### 7.1 Creating a Payroll Run
1. Navigate to **H.R. Command → Pay Cycle Studio**.
2. Click **Create Payroll Run**.
3. Select:
   - **Period Start** and **Period End** dates
   - **Department** (or company-wide)
4. The system pulls attendance data and computes preliminary salary figures.
5. Review the **Payroll Summary Table**:
   - Employee name, base salary, deductions, additions, net pay
6. Click **Lock Attendance** to freeze the attendance data for this run.
7. Review any **exceptions** (missing clock-ins, disputed leave).
8. Click **Submit to FlowGate** → routes to Finance Manager for approval.

### 7.2 Approving a Payroll Run (Finance Manager)
1. Go to **Workflow Inbox** and find the payroll approval request.
2. Review the full payroll summary.
3. Click **Approve (Finance)** → payroll is cleared for payment.
4. Click **Export Journal** → generates the payroll journal entry for the Finance ledger.

### 7.3 Payslip Studio (`/core/hr/payslip`)
1. Navigate to **Payslip Studio**.
2. Search for an employee and a pay period.
3. Click **Generate Payslip** → creates a formatted PDF payslip.
4. Click **Send to Employee** → emails the payslip to the employee.

## 8. Case Vault — HR Cases (`/core/hr/cases`)

**Purpose:** Manage HR cases — disputes, grievances, disciplinary actions, and corrections.

### 8.1 Creating a New Case
1. Navigate to **H.R. Command → Case Vault**.
2. Click **New Case**.
3. Fill in:
   - **Case Type** (Grievance, Disciplinary, Correction, Query)
   - **Employee Involved**
   - **Case Description**
   - **Priority** (Low, Medium, High, Urgent)
4. Click **Submit Case**.

### 8.2 Assigning a Case Owner
1. Open the case from the **Active Cases** table.
2. Click **Assign Owner**.
3. Select an HR staff member from the dropdown.
4. Click **Confirm Assignment**.
5. The assigned owner is notified.

### 8.3 Escalating a Case
1. Open the case.
2. Click **Escalate**.
3. Select the escalation target: **HR Manager**, **Legal**, or **Executive**.
4. Add escalation notes.
5. Click **Submit Escalation** → creates a FlowGate request.

### 8.4 Tracking Case Resolution
1. In the case detail view, the **Activity Timeline** shows all actions taken.
2. Add **Resolution Notes** as the case progresses.
3. When resolved, click **Mark as Resolved**.
4. The case is archived and an audit entry is created.

## 9. Workflow — FlowGate (`/core/workflow?scope=HR`)

**Purpose:** HR's approval inbox — view and action all pending HR workflow requests.

### Approving a Request
1. Navigate to **Workflow → FlowGate** (scoped to HR).
2. All pending approvals are listed.
3. Click a request to open the full detail.
4. Review the request: who submitted it, what it's for, relevant notes.
5. Click **Approve**, **Reject**, or **Request Modification**.
6. A notification is sent to the original requester.

> **Every approval or rejection creates an immutable audit log entry.**

## 10. Lex Board — Legal & Contracts (`/core/hr/legal`)

**Purpose:** Legal compliance, employment contracts, visa tracking, and regulatory obligations.

### 10.1 Creating an Employment Contract
1. Navigate to **H.R. Command → Lex Board**.
2. Click **Create Contract**.
3. Select:
   - **Employee**
   - **Contract Type** (Permanent, Fixed-Term, Probationary, Freelance)
   - **Start Date** and **End Date** (if applicable)
   - **Contract Terms** (attach document or fill template)
4. Click **Save Contract**.
5. Click **Send to Employee for Signature** → routes to employee via FlowGate.

### 10.2 Renewing a Contract
1. Find an expiring contract in the **Expiring Soon** list.
2. Click **Request Renewal**.
3. Update terms if needed.
4. Click **Route to FlowGate** → sends to Department Head and HR Manager for approval.

### 10.3 Visa and Work Permit Tracking
1. In the **Visa Tracker** panel, all foreign national employees with work permits are listed.
2. Expiry dates are highlighted:
   - 🟡 Yellow = expiring within 90 days
   - 🔴 Red = expiring within 30 days or expired
3. Click **Initiate Renewal** to start the renewal workflow.

## 11. Administration (`/core/hr/admin`)

**Purpose:** HR configuration — working calendars, leave policies, allowance rules, and department settings.

### Managing Leave Policies
1. Navigate to **H.R. Command → Administration**.
2. In the **Leave Policy** section, click **New Policy**.
3. Set: **Leave Type** (Annual, Sick, Emergency), **Annual Entitlement** (days), **Carryover Rules**.
4. Click **Save Policy**.

### Configuring Working Calendar
1. In the **Working Calendar** section, set:
   - **Working Days** (e.g., Monday–Friday)
   - **Working Hours** (e.g., 08:00–17:00)
   - **Public Holidays** (add each national/local holiday)
2. Click **Save Calendar**.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|---------|
| Employee record not found | Check search filters — try employee code instead of name |
| Cannot terminate an employee | Ensure you have HR Admin or Owner role |
| Payroll run shows incorrect salary | Update the employee's salary in Edit Profile first |
| FlowGate approval not appearing | Verify the destination department is set to HR |
| Training completion not updating | Ask the employee to mark completion in their profile |

---

## Best Practices

- ✅ Always route terminations and transfers through FlowGate — never manually delete records.
- ✅ Lock attendance before creating a payroll run to prevent data changes.
- ✅ Review expiring contracts and visas weekly using the Lex Board.
- ✅ Use Case Vault for all HR disputes — maintain a written record.
- ✅ Keep People Core records current — payroll accuracy depends on it.
